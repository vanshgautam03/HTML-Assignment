    About Us

    Welcome to V's Pizza World, where we believe that pizza is not just a food, but an experience. We started in 2003 with a small pizza shop in Barrie, and since then, we have grown to become one of the most popular pizza destinations in the city. We take great pride in using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients to make our pizzas, which are hand-tossed and baked to perfection in our wood-fired ovens. At V's Pizza World, we don't just make pizza, we create memories. Whether you're here for a quick lunch or a leisurely dinner, we want you to feel at home and enjoy our delicious pizzas with your friends and family. Our friendly staff is always here to assist you in choosing the perfect pizza and beverage pairing to make your dining experience truly memorable.

    So come and visit us today at one of our locations or make an order here for delivery at your location, and let us show you why V's Pizza World is the ultimate destination for pizza lovers.
